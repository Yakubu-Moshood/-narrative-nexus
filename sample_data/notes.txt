Team Meeting Notes - Market Expansion Strategy
Date: December 15, 2025

Attendees: CEO, Sales Lead, Operations Manager, Marketing Director

Discussion Summary:

The team discussed our market expansion strategy for Q1 2026. The consensus was strong: we should focus exclusively on Lagos for our expansion efforts.

CEO: "Lagos is booming. The urban market is where our customers are. We've seen tremendous success there. Lagos is the only market that makes sense right now."

Sales Lead: "I agree completely. Lagos has been our strongest performer. The urban demographic is perfect for our product. Lagos expansion is the obvious choice. We should double down on Lagos."

Operations Manager: "The infrastructure in Lagos is better. Urban areas have better logistics. Lagos has everything we need for growth."

Marketing Director: "Our brand resonates in urban centers. Lagos is where the trend-setters are. Urban markets are safer bets. Lagos should be our priority."

CEO: "Exactly. The data shows Lagos is booming. Urban expansion is the way to go. Let's commit all resources to Lagos."

Sales Lead: "The numbers don't lie - Lagos is our best market. Urban customers are more valuable. We should skip other regions entirely."

Team Consensus: Focus exclusively on Lagos. The urban market is our sweet spot. No resources for other regions. Lagos expansion is the clear winner.

Action Items:
1. Allocate 100% of Q1 budget to Lagos operations
2. Hire 5 new sales reps for Lagos office
3. Launch Lagos-focused marketing campaign
4. Expand warehouse in Lagos

Risk Assessment: Minimal risk in Lagos. Urban market is stable and growing.
